# Habits that save time

## Have a to-do list for the next day

- Having a todo list for the following day helps with [[Decision Fatigue]].
	- Makes doing hard things and taking decisions easier.

# Having a limited wardrobe

- Wearing the same thing every day will help you save time in the morning.
	- [[Steve Jobs]] and [[Mark Zuckerberg]] are known for this.

# Declutter and organise

- Having a clean and organised workspace will help you save time.
	- You will spend less time looking for things.
- Things that take space in your home, takes up space in your brain

# Unsubscribe from emails and newsletters

- It will not interrupt you and you will not have to spend time deleting them.

# Delete unnecessary apps

- No need for apps that you don't use.
  - They take up space and time.

# [[Automation|Automate]] everything you can

- Don't go too far
- Make sure you have not tied yourself into an ecosystem as well

# Have [[Routine|routines]]

- Do your chores before bed
- You will not be stressed in the morning

# Go for the [[Low Hanging Fruits]] first

- If something takes 5 minutes, do it now.
- The longer you store a task in your brain, the more pressure you feel
- If it's a longer task, brain dump it to a to-do list or something.

# Create [[Standards Of Practice|SOPs]]

- If you do something more than once, create a standard for it.

- Saves time and money

# Delegate tasks that are lower than your hourly rate

- [[Time is money]] and you should spend it wisely.
- If you can pay someone to do something for less than you make in an hour, do it.
