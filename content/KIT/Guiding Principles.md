1. [[KIT/Linking Your Thinking]]
	- Your brain uses relations and links to learn, remember and understand and so should your vault.
2. Your Vault is your garden and it needs maintenance, but don't overdo it. 
	- Make sure to weed your garden, but don't do it unless you encounter a note/idea. Going through your notes just for the sake of maintaining them is not the best idea.
3. Order from chaos
	- Don't think too much about where you are putting notes, rather focus on capturing data.
4. Dynamicity of data
	- Data should be dynamically retrievable. Utilizing the [[KIT/YAML Front Matter\|YAML Frontmatter]] as well as other aspects of a note.
