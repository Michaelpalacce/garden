> [!success] ~ What ? ~
> **P**ersonal **K**nowledge **M**anagement is a process of **storing information** that will help you **make meaningful connections** and **assist you** in **extracting it** when the time comes.

The most important part of having your own pkm is that it **should be working for you**. If you find yourself taking notes, just for the sake of [[KIT/Note Taking]], you need to rethink why you are actually making a PKM.

My reason for taking notes is to [[KIT/How To Retain Knowledge\|retain knowledge]].
