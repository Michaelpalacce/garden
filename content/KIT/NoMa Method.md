# Difference Between Note-Taking and Note-Making

[[KIT/Note Taking]] is when notes are easy to go in and easy to go out, usually taken from the world around you directly, while [[KIT/Note Making]] is when notes are created for a reason, explained in your own words.

# 5 Prompts

- Notice when you say: *"That's Interesting"*
	- You've found out something worth exploring
- Instead of collecting it, you say: *"That reminds me"*
	- it gets you into an active mode of thinking
	- Answer the questions
		- It's important because
		- It's interesting because
		- It's similar because
		- It's different because

At the end of the process, make sure to collect your ideas in the daily note and [[KIT/How To Retain Knowledge\|retain]] at the end.
