> [!info]+
> **Standards of Practice** (SoP) in software engineering and IT are **established guidelines or norms** that **outline best practices, ethical considerations, technical standards, and operational procedures** for professionals in the field. These standards ensure that **work is carried out consistently, efficiently, and safely**, while also **complying with legal, regulatory, and organizational requirements**.
