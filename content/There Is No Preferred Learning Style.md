> [!info]+
> People will claim that they are a **"visual", "auditory", "verbal" or a "written" learner**, but there is **no such thing**. When learning you should **try to engage as many senses as possible** to allow your **brain to successfully process information**.

# The Brain Can [[Multitasking\|Multitask]]

**Different parts of the brain can work at the same time efficiently**. Utilize this capability to capture more information.
