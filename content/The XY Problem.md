> [!info]+
> **A person comes to you for help with a solution to a problem (Y), instead of the actual problem they are trying to solve**.

This can **lead to misunderstandings** because the focus is on **fixing the wrong thing**.

To avoid this, **it's better to describe the original problem** (X) and let others help find the best solution, rather than assuming the approach (Y) is correct.
